Web 2.0

Web 2.0 is a term that was popularized back in 2004. It showcased the advancements past its predecessor in every way. There was more functionality, a more refined client and server, a bigger emphasis on communication. Social media such as YouTube, MySpace, Facebook, etc, were developed at this time. Businesses and services became present on the web as services such as Amazon, Google, or Wikipedia at this time as well. There was a massive increase in user participation that enriched the overall user experience on the Web.
