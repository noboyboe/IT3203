Web 2.0

Web 2.0 is a term that was popularized back in 2004. It showcased the advancements past its predecessor in every way. There was more functionality, a more refined client and server, a bigger emphasis on communication. Social media such as YouTube, MySpace, Facebook, etc, were developed at this time.
